    • Education and counseling related to contraceptives and sterilization
    • Certain sterilization procedures

    Your contraceptives-only plan reimburses for federally-mandated women's contraceptive services. These services are reimbursed at 100% if received from an in-network provider.

    Covered services include:

    • Education and counseling related to contraceptives and sterilization
    • Certain sterilization procedures
    • The insertion or removal of, as well as any medically necessary examinations associated with the use of, intrauterine devices, diaphragms, injectable contraceptives and implanted hormonal contraceptives
    • Contraceptive drugs and devices when coverage is mandated by federal law (prescription required)

    The following conditions must be met for these services to be reimbursed:

    • Services are designated as women's contraceptive services under federal law. (See bcbsnc.com/preventive for up-to-date information.)
    • Services are performed by an in-network provider, unless otherwise specified.
    • Services are provided in an office-based, outpatient or ambulatory setting or urgent care center. (Note: Tubal ligations may be reimbursed when performed inpatient.)

    No reimbursement will be provided for out-of-network contraceptive services, with the exception of contraceptive drugs and devices.
